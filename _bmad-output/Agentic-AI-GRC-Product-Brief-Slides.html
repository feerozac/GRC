Agentic AI GRC Platform

Product Brief Summary

Project: agent-grc · Author: Mark · Date: 2026-01-29

Vision

  • AI-native Governance, Risk & Compliance platform: single source of truth for controls, risks, evidence
  • Virtual 3 lines of defence (3LOD) built in
  • Real-time / near real-time regulatory development (HKMA, MAS, others)
  • Review of NIST, ISO, COBIT frameworks; org chart & process-to-org mapping
  • Annual reports & strategy docs → governance issues (COBIT, COSO)
  • KCI, KRI, KPI module linked to governance, process, regulatory, policies/standards; gap & metric suggestions
  • AI agents: narratives, gap analysis, evidence mapping, KCI/KRI/KPI discussion & priority recommendations (strategy, regulatory, industry benchmarks)

Problem

  • Fragmented risk registers, control libraries, issue trackers, evidence across spreadsheets and point tools
  • Multi-framework mapping (ISO 27001, NIST CSF, COBIT, HKMA, MAS) manual and brittle
  • Regulatory requirements (HKMA, MAS) often discovered late; framework updates not systematically reviewed
  • 3LOD work in silos; challenge and assurance inconsistent and hard to evidence
  • Process/control ownership misaligned to org; governance issues identified ad hoc
  • KCI/KRI/KPI siloed; weak linkage; no strategy/benchmark-driven prioritisation

Solution — Key Capabilities

  • Risk register + Control library (ISO 27001, NIST CSF, COBIT, HKMA, MAS)
  • Real-time regulatory development; Framework review (NIST, ISO, COBIT)
  • Issue & remediation tracking; AI executive narratives; AI gap analysis
  • Evidence mapping; Org chart reading & process-to-org mapping
  • Annual report & strategy → governance issues (COBIT, COSO)
  • KCI/KRI/KPI module: linkage to governance, process, regulatory, policies/standards; gap & metric suggestions; agent priority recommendations
  • Virtual 3LOD: line-specific views, challenge flow, reporting

Differentiators

  • Agentic AI: proactive narratives, gap analysis, evidence mapping
  • Virtual 3LOD: first-class 1L/2L/3L model
  • Multi-framework by design (ISO 27001, NIST CSF, COBIT, HKMA, MAS); framework review
  • Real-time regulatory intelligence (HKMA, MAS)
  • Evidence-centric; Org-driven mapping; Document-driven governance (annual reports, strategy → COBIT/COSO)
  • KCI/KRI/KPI with agent-driven priorities (strategy, regulatory, benchmarks)

Virtual 3LOD in the Product

  • First line (1L): My controls, evidence, issues, attestations
  • Second line (2L): Risk & control oversight; challenge status; framework coverage; KCI/KRI/KPI and priorities; open issues across 1L
  • Third line (3L): Audit plan; control testing; findings; assurance opinion
  • Visibility and workflows scoped by line (and entity); separation clear for regulators and board

Non-Functional Requirements (Summary)

  • Performance: 10K risks, 5K controls, 20K issues (< 3 s p95); narrative < 60 s; gap analysis < 5 min
  • Regulatory: Real-time/near real-time regulatory ingestion (e.g. 24 h); NIST/ISO/COBIT review; org chart & process-to-org; annual report/strategy → governance; KCI/KRI/KPI module with agent priorities
  • Security: Encryption (TLS 1.3, AES-256); RBAC by line of defence; 7-year tamper-evident audit log
  • Availability: 99.5%; RPO ≤ 24 h, RTO ≤ 4 h
  • Usability: Modern browsers; WCAG 2.1 AA for core journeys
  • AI: Reviewable/versioned narratives; auditable gap analysis & evidence mapping; 3LOD views & reporting; APIs; extensible frameworks
